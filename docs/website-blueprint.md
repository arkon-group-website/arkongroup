## Homepage goal

Make ARKON Group feel like a Saudi multidisciplinary project support ecosystem, not just a normal company profile.

Homepage must:
- establish trust in 5 seconds
- show the five companies clearly
- communicate capability across the project lifecycle
- drive proposal requests
- support English and Arabic SEO
- remain fast despite premium 3D visuals
